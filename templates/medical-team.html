Medical Ethics Review Board

Ensuring responsible and ethical AI healthcare guidance

Our Medical Ethics Review Board provides oversight and guidance on the ethical implications of AI in healthcare. This independent body reviews our protocols, training data, and system outputs to ensure LLM Doctor adheres to the highest standards of medical ethics.

Ethical Oversight Functions

  • Regular review of AI model responses and recommendations
  • Assessment of medical information accuracy and relevance
  • Monitoring for potential biases in healthcare guidance
  • Development of ethical guidelines for AI healthcare information
  • Oversight of privacy practices and data security protocols

The board consists of medical ethicists, healthcare policy experts, patient advocates, and clinical practitioners who meet quarterly to review LLM Doctor's operations and provide recommendations for improvement.
